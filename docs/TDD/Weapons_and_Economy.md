# Chapter 1: Weapons & Economy Manifest

**Related Files:** `project_overview.md`, `Chapter-1/Map_Chapter-1`

**Overview:** This document defines the technological origins, Scrap economy, and geographical distribution of the player's arsenal. Weapons are divided between fixed Wallbuys (scaled by map depth) and the randomized 3D Fabricator pool.

---

## 1. Technological Origins
The arsenal reflects the history of the Great Divide, separated into three distinct mechanical archetypes:

*   **Human Kinetics (The Resistance Arsenal):** Scavenged, century-old ballistic weaponry. High raw kinetic impact. Excels at physical stagger and crowd control but struggles to pierce late-game Ablative Plasma-Plating.
*   **Machine Energy (Stolen Tech):** Weapons ripped directly from destroyed Vanguard units. Pure thermal damage that melts through heavy armor effortlessly, but runs on finite battery cells and features risky, slow venting reload animations.
*   **Vampire Bio-Tech (The Spliced Arsenal):** The horrific synthesis of machinery and immortal biology custom-built by the Exiles. Fires crystallized blood or corrosive bile. Functions on utility, damage-over-time, and Vitae interaction.

## 2. Wallbuy Economy (The 11 Tiers)
Wallbuy locations are fixed, and their Scrap costs scale with geographical depth and enemy difficulty.

**Zone 1: The Decontamination Hub (Early Game)**
*   **Tier 1 (500 Scrap):** *Scrap-Pistol* (Human Kinetic) - Weak starting weapon, pays for itself quickly.
*   **Tier 2 (750 Scrap):** *Trench-Gun* (Human Kinetic) - Pump-action, reliable for early Scrap-Walkers.

**Zone 2A / 2B: The Bio-Wing & Industrial Wing (Mid Game)**
*   **Tier 3 (1000 Scrap):** *Rivet-Repeater* (Human Kinetic) - High fire rate, low accuracy SMG. *(Zone 2B)*
*   **Tier 4 (1250 Scrap):** *Defoliant Sprayer* (Bio-Tech) - Short-range acid flamethrower. *(Zone 2A)*
*   **Tier 5 (1500 Scrap):** *Kinetic DMR* (Human Kinetic) - High-damage, single-shot rifle for catwalk engagements. *(Zone 2B)*
*   **Tier 6 (1750 Scrap):** *Ion-Burst Pistol* (Machine Energy) - Three-round burst, highly effective against early armor. *(Zone 2B)*
*   **Tier 7 (2000 Scrap):** *Mag-Rail Shotgun* (Human Kinetic) - Devastating close-range stagger capability. *(Zone 2A)*

**Zone 3: The Mag-Train Platform (Late Game)**
*   **Tier 8 (2250 Scrap):** *Plasma-Cutter Auto-Rifle* (Machine Energy) - Heavy sustained thermal damage.
*   **Tier 9 (2500 Scrap):** *Aegis-Breaker* (Bio-Tech) - Auto-shotgun firing corrosive bile shells.
*   **Tier 10 (2750 Scrap):** *Heavy Mass-Driver* (Human Kinetic) - LMG with massive penetration for the final holdout.
*   **Tier 11 (3000 Scrap):** *Vanguard Cannon* (Machine Energy) - Heavy explosive plasma rounds; significantly reduces player movement speed while equipped.

## 3. The 3D Fabricator Pool (Randomized Arsenal)
The Fabricator acts as the "Mystery Box," costing a flat 950 Scrap per roll. It pulls from a randomized pool of 13 weapon prints, categorized by reliability and mechanical origin.

### The 3 Duds (The Gamble)
These prints are broken, outdated, or highly situational. Pulling one early can severely punish a player's Scrap economy.
*   **The Relic Musket (Human Kinetic):** A century-old, lever-action hunting rifle from the early days of the resistance. It deals massive headshot damage but has an abysmal two-second rechamber time between every single shot, making it a death sentence against swarms.
*   **Fractured Plasma-Torch (Machine Energy):** A severely damaged Vanguard cutting tool. It melts armor instantly at point-blank range, but overheats after just 1.5 seconds of sustained fire. Venting the heat takes too long and leaves the player completely defenseless.
*   **The Sickened Leech (Bio-Tech):** Fires slow-moving, parasitic blobs that attach to machines and drain their health. The damage-over-time is incredibly slow, meaning the machine will likely still close the distance and hit the player before it actually dies.

### The 7 Workhorses (The Reliable Core)
These are the backbone of the mid-to-late game. They are highly reliable weapons from all three tech origins that reward skilled play and Bio-Kinetic movement.
*   **Liberator AR (Human Kinetic):** The standard-issue, 60-round resistance assault rifle. It lacks armor penetration but features incredibly high stagger-rounds, perfect for keeping hordes at bay while backing down a hallway.
*   **Twin Vipers (Human Kinetic):** Dual-wielded, high-RPM submachine guns. You cannot aim down sights with them, but they grant a slight boost to movement and sliding speed, making them the ultimate run-and-gun weapons.
*   **Phase-Piercer (Machine Energy):** A heavy energy sniper rifle. Instead of firing projectiles, it shoots a continuous, concentrated beam for one second that punches through an infinite number of enemies in a perfectly straight line.
*   **Volt-Caster (Machine Energy):** A rapid-fire, compact energy SMG. It shreds Vanguard Ablative Armor but requires precise burst-firing. If the player empties the magazine completely, the forced thermal-venting reload animation takes 3 seconds longer.
*   **Spore-Lobber (Bio-Tech):** A modified grenade launcher that fires clustered sacs of toxic Acheron flora. The sacs detonate into localized pools of highly corrosive acid, making it the perfect area-of-denial weapon for locking down tight doorways.
*   **Osteo-Carbine (Bio-Tech):** A tactical burst-rifle that fires hyper-dense, calcified bone shards. If a shard strikes an unarmored target, it shatters on impact, causing explosive splinter damage to immediately adjacent enemies.
*   **Mag-Saw Launcher (Industrial Hybrid):** A repurposed mining tool that fires heavy, spinning industrial saw blades. The blades natively ricochet off walls up to three times, allowing players to clear blind corners or flood tight spaces with bouncing death.

### The 3 Wonder Weapons (The Ultimate Arsenal)
Ultra-rare, overpowered experimental tech. Pulling one of these dramatically shifts the odds of survival.
*   **The Singularity Projector (Machine Tech):** A miniaturized warp-drive engine. Fires a collapsed gravity sphere that detonates into a localized black hole, instantly crushing weak enemies and helplessly rooting heavy Vanguards in place for 5 seconds.
*   **The Hemophage Symbiote (Bio-Tech):** A living parasitic organism that grafts onto the player's arm. Fires homing bone-spines with perfect accuracy and has infinite ammo. **Drawback:** Reloading actively drains the player's Vitae meter (or shield segments if Vitae is empty).
*   **The Arc-Storm Engine (Acheron Tech):** A shoulder-mounted cannon utilizing environmental tech. Fires chain lightning that jumps between machines, multiplying its damage with every jump. The ultimate anti-swarm tool.

*(Note: The map's 4th Wonder Weapon, the **Acheron Spewer**, is strictly excluded from the Fabricator pool and can only be obtained by completing the Bio-Canister Synthesis Easter Egg).*
