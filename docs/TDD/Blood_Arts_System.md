# Chapter 1: Blood Arts & Vitae System

**Related Files:** `project_overview.md`, `Chapter-1/Map_Chapter-1`

**Overview:** Blood Arts are high-impact, tactical utility abilities unique to the bio-hacked biology of the Exiles. They are designed to break line-of-sight, manipulate AI pathing, or create escape windows during late-game survival scenarios.

---

## 1. The Vitae Economy (Risk vs. Reward)
*   **The Mechanic:** Vitae is the internal resource required to trigger a Blood Art. It does not passively regenerate over time, nor can it be purchased with Scrap.
*   **The Aggression Loop:** Vitae is strictly earned through high-risk, close-quarters combat. Players must execute melee kills, perform point-blank shotgun executions, or use the Momentum Slide to pass directly through destroyed machines to absorb lingering airborne bio-matter.
*   **Tactical Impact:** This economy forces players who are cornered in late rounds to push aggressively *forward* into danger to earn their survival abilities, preventing infinite backward kiting.

---

## 2. The Exile Kits (Unique Blood Arts)
All six playable characters share identical base stats, hitboxes, and weapon handling. However, each Exile spawns with a unique Blood Art specifically tied to their lore and corporate/exile history.

*   **Swapping Arts:** While players spawn with their lore-specific Blood Art, they can swap to any other character's ability mid-match by spending 2,000 Scrap at the "Vitae Splicer" terminal located back in the Decontamination Hub.

### The Aristocrat: *Founder’s Edict*
*   **Lore:** Former corporate CEO who bio-hacked their blood to carry legacy admin access codes.
*   **Mechanic:** Physically injects a biometric admin-override into a machine's optical sensor.
*   **Tactical Edge (Aggro Redirection):** The hacked machine's IFF resets, recognizing the Aristocrat as its "Creator." For 8 seconds, the machine (even heavy Vanguards) turns its weapons on the horde, acting as a personal bodyguard and pulling all enemy aggro.

### The Bio-Hacker: *Marrow-Rust*
*   **Lore:** The medic/splicer who engineered flesh to consume metal.
*   **Mechanic:** Ruptures a vein to release a localized cloud of airborne, corrosive bio-nanites.
*   **Tactical Edge (Armor Stripping):** Instantly corrodes the Ablative Plasma-Plating off any Vanguard machine that walks through the cloud, permanently turning bullet-sponge elites into highly vulnerable, unarmored targets for the squad.

### The Sympathizer: *EMP-Spliced Vitae*
*   **Lore:** Fought alongside the human-machine alliance, heavily interwoven with illegal cybernetics.
*   **Mechanic:** Supercharges the iron in their blood, releasing a localized, bio-magnetic EMP blast.
*   **Tactical Edge (Mobility Control):** Paralyzes machine servos and kills the magnetic adhesion of Scrap-Walkers. Any machine crawling on the walls or ceiling violently drops to the floor, instantly clearing vertical escape routes for a Wall-Kick.

### The Ancient: *Coagulated Wraith*
*   **Lore:** Adapted the ancient myth of "turning to mist" to fool AI sensors.
*   **Mechanic:** Expels super-heated, coagulated blood to form a static physical silhouette, simultaneously dropping their own core body temperature to absolute zero.
*   **Tactical Edge (Thermal Invisibility):** Machine thermal optics instantly lock onto the burning-hot blood decoy. The Ancient becomes invisible to tracking systems for 4 seconds, allowing them to casually walk past firing squads.

### The Feral: *Apex Ossification*
*   **Lore:** Shed the corporate suit and pushed physical mutations to the absolute limit.
*   **Mechanic:** Extends jagged, bio-reinforced bone blades to impale a weak machine, draining its coolant directly to soothe their overheating Vitae.
*   **Tactical Edge (Unstoppable Momentum):** Triggers "Ossification" for 3 seconds. The Feral gains infinite mass, cannot be staggered, and can physically sprint directly *through* heavy machines like a bowling ball to break out of a dead-end.

### The Narrator: *Acheron Resonance*
*   **Lore:** The architect of the exile who studied the magnetic storms of Acheron-9.
*   **Mechanic:** Bio-hacks vocal cords to emit a piercing acoustic frequency that mimics the planet's chaotic magnetic storms.
*   **Tactical Edge (Sensor Scrambling):** Violently scrambles the targeting arrays of any machine in the audio radius. Enemies fire blindly in 360 degrees, creating a massive zone of chaotic crossfire that heavily damages the horde itself.
