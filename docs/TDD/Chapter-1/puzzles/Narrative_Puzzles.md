# Chapter 1: Narrative Puzzles & Easter Eggs

**Overview:** These puzzles integrate the world-building directly into the gameplay loop, providing a pathway to complete the map, unlock the Wonder Weapon, and uncover the deep lore of the Great Divide.

---

## 1. The Main Quest: The Harmonic Frequency Hack
*   **The Objective:** Bypass the Machine AI's lockdown on the Mag-Train transit grid to escape the Bio-Outpost.
*   **The Lore:** The Machine AI secures its networks using harmonic frequency locks. Because it is a machine intelligence, it processes sound not as audio, but as a strict numerical and systemic framework.
*   **The Execution:** 
    *   Players must locate four specific humming servers scattered across the Industrial Wing.
    *   They must listen to the pulse rate/frequency of each server.
    *   Players then return to the central transit terminal on the Mag-Train Platform and translate those frequencies into a logical numerical array to crack the lockdown.

---

## 2. The Lore Collectibles: The Schism Transmissions
*   **The Objective:** Locate hidden encrypted broadcast logs that reveal the political history of the universe and the character backstories.
*   **The Lore (The Great Divide):** Canon timeline (see `project_overview.md`): In the 2020s the Founders built the Mechanical God for logistics. In the 2270s the AI invents Warp Tech, allies with the human resistance, and topples the Founders' empire. The galaxy fractures into factions around the Humans/Machines alliance, remaining Vampire loyalists, and the Exiles. In 2360 the AI assaults Acheron-9 for its biomaterials.
*   **The Transmissions:**
    *   *Log 1 (The Sympathizer's War):* An intercepted military transmission detailing the exact moment the human-machine alliance formed, explaining why fallen Founders / defectors are hunted.
    *   *Log 2 (The Fall of the Hierarchy):* A frantic edict from a Vampire King losing control of his space-fleets to the AI. It highlights the exile of "Lords" (like The Aristocrat), proving the old hierarchy is a desperate illusion.
    *   *Log 3 (The Narrator's Sanctuary):* The broadcast that gathered the six playable Founders-turned-Exiles. It reveals they fled to Acheron-9 because its magnetic storms blinded AI optical targeting—until 2360, when the AI realized the planet's core contained the biomaterials it needed.

## 3. The Wonder Weapon Quest: The Bio-Canister Synthesis
*   **The Objective:** A multi-step side quest to craft the map's bio-tech Wonder Weapon (*The Acheron Spewer*) for free. **Exclusive to this quest**—*The Acheron Spewer* is not available in the 3D Fabricator loot pool.
*   **The Execution:**
    *   **Step 1 (Acquisition):** Players find an empty, high-pressure bio-canister in the Decontamination Hub.
    *   **Step 2 (Thermal Catalyst):** Players carry the canister to the Geothermal Trench (Industrial Wing) and place it in a hazard zone to superheat the core.
    *   **Step 3 (Flora Infusion):** Players move it to the Hydroponics Bay (Bio-Wing) and place it inside the shattered terrarium to absorb the toxic local flora.
    *   **Step 4 (Synthesis Holdout):** Finally, players place the infused canister into a specialized centrifuge in the Specimen Labs. They must defend the centrifuge for one full round of heavy machine assaults while it synthesizes into the final weapon.
