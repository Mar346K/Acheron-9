# Chapter 1 Finale: The Magnetic Decoupling

**Overview:** The ultimate holdout sequence. After completing the Harmonic Frequency Hack, players must manually decouple the Mag-Train from the platform while surviving the highest density of machine spawns in the chapter.

---

## The Trigger & The Layout
*   **The Activation:** Inputting the correct numerical array into the central terminal powers the Mag-Train. The facility's emergency strobes activate, signaling the AI Director's lockdown sequence.
*   **The Obstacle:** The train is held to the transit floor by three massive, heavy-industrial magnetic clamps spread across the cavern to prevent corner-camping.
*   **Clamp 1 (The Bio-Lock):** Located on the transit floor near the Bio-Wing entrance. Highly susceptible to ground-level Scrap-Walker swarms.
*   **Clamp 2 (The Forge-Lock):** Located near the Industrial Wing. Defended by heavy Vanguard units pouring from adjacent loading docks.
*   **Clamp 3 (The Array-Lock):** Located on the third-story balcony of the Power Array, requiring vertical traversal to access.

---

## The Hacking Mechanics (Scaling Difficulty)
*   **The Override Zones:** Each clamp features a glowing circular radius at its base. Players must stand within this zone to progress the decoupling hack.
*   **Squad Play (Divide and Conquer):** The hacking process is slow. The global decoupling multiplier increases based on how many unique zones are occupied simultaneously (e.g., 3 players in 3 different zones = 3x speed). 
*   **Contested Zones:** If a machine steps inside an occupied zone, the progress bar flashes yellow and pauses entirely until the machine is eliminated, forcing players to actively defend their circle.
*   **Solo Play (The Gauntlet):** The AI scales the encounter. The solo player hacks one clamp at a time sequentially. The override speed is drastically faster, but the player must hold their ground against the entire map's aggregated spawn pool.

---

## The Climax & Transition
*   **The Unleashed Swarm:** As the final clamp releases, the cavern violently shakes. The AI Director removes the spawn cap, flooding the room with an overwhelming, endless wall of machines.
*   **The Boarding Phase:** A 15-second countdown blares. The heavy armored doors of the Mag-Train finally slide open.
*   **The Escape:** Players must abandon the override zones and utilize Bio-Kinetic Flow (sliding, vault-boosting) to break through the horde and physically board the train before the timer expires.
*   **The Cliffhanger:** At zero, the doors slam shut. Any player left on the platform is instantly downed. The train violently accelerates into a vertical launch tube, fading to black to the sound of grinding metal (setting up the Chapter 2 derailment).
