# Chapter 1, Zone 2A: The Bio-Wing (Architectural Blueprint)

**Zone Purpose:** A high-speed, momentum-based arena that introduces players to environmental hazards and vertical escapes. It transitions the player from the claustrophobic spawn into the hostile biological hazard environment.

---

## Room 1: The Overgrown Corridor (The Vertical Bypass)
*   **Tactical Purpose:** A tight transition bottleneck. 
*   **Atmosphere:** Industrial metal walls buckling inward, crushed by massive, glowing green roots. Failing amber emergency strobes mix with sickly bio-luminescence.
*   **Interactables:** The Mag-Rail Shotgun wallbuy is mounted on the inner corner of the L-shape.
*   **The Vampire Advantage (Root Canopy):** The toxic roots form a dense, elevated mesh near the ceiling, absorbing enemy plasma fire.
*   **The Escape Loop:** If trapped between heavy Vanguard units, players use the Wall-Kick to launch into the root canopy. Chaining jumps off the elevated roots allows players to bypass the machines fighting on the floor and drop safely on the other side.
*   **Machine Ingress:** Floor grates and wall vents heavily obscured by hanging vines, requiring players to rely on the audio cues of tearing metal.

---

## Room 2: The Hydroponics Bay (The Spore Smokescreen)
*   **Tactical Purpose:** The primary kiting arena, built for wide-circle momentum slides.
*   **Atmosphere:** A humid, sprawling, ruined greenhouse. The floor is slick with condensation and crushed plant matter under a shattered glass ceiling.
*   **Interactables:** Defoliant Sprayer wallbuy (left wall), 3D Fabricator spawn point (right alcove), Corrosive Sprinklers trap (ceiling).
*   **The Vampire Advantage (Sightline Manipulation):** The central shattered terrarium acts as a deep trench players can slide through for heavy 360-degree hard cover.
*   **The Escape Loop:** Players can shoot glowing Acheron Spore-Pods to release a localized cloud of thick bio-gas. Machine optical sensors are blinded by the gas, disabling their ranged weapons, allowing vampires to melt them at close range.
*   **Machine Ingress:** Agile Hunter-Killers shatter the glass ceiling and drop directly into the kiting path, forcing dynamic route changes.

---

## Room 3: The Specimen Labs (The Vault-Maze)
*   **Tactical Purpose:** A high-risk reward room that holds a vital perk but is highly cluttered.
*   **Atmosphere:** Chaotic and clinical. Overturned stainless-steel surgical tables, shattered datapads, and a large broken specimen incubator litter the space.
*   **Interactables:** Vampiric Stride perk centrifuge, a 3D Fabricator spawn point, Pheromone Vent traps, and Gate C (the exit bulkhead).
*   **The Vampire Advantage (Pathfinding Exploitation):** The un-shatterable surgical tables form a jagged, maze-like grid providing perfect waist-high cover against plasma fire.
*   **The Escape Loop:** Heavy Vanguard machines cannot jump or shatter the tables, forcing them to slowly walk the maze. Vampires use the Vault-Boost to launch directly over multiple tables, trapping the slow machines deep in the clutter.
